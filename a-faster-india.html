A Faster India

25 Feb 2023 · Work

Oh my gosh, I can't believe it! In November 2022, we were awarded the contract to build a brand new container for the first bullet train track between Mumbai and Ahmedabad! I am beyond excited to be a crucial part of this groundbreaking project, and I can't wait to see our capabilities shine.

Let me tell you, this container is no ordinary piece of equipment. It is specifically designed to transport and store all the necessary equipment and materials required for welding the bullet train track. How cool is that? But wait, there's more! The container is also able to transform into a mobile welding station through the use of innovative modular components. Can you believe it?

I'm telling you, this is some cutting-edge technology we're dealing with here. The container is able to sit on a rail-and-road truck and move to weld the tracks that have been laid, through a robotic arm. This means that the track can be welded on-site without any additional transportation of equipment and materials. It's like something out of a science fiction movie!

Overall, I am just so proud to be part of this innovative project. Our container is a significant step forward in the construction of bullet train tracks in India. With its portability, cutting-edge technology, and safety features, I know it is going to play a critical role in the success of this landmark project.

I can't wait to see the results of the bullet train track when it is completed. It's going to be a game-changer for transportation in India, and I'm just so grateful to be a part of it.
